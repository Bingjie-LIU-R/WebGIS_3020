More On Me

About Childhood

I was born in Quanfuhe Village, Yuguan Town, Wufeng Tujia Autonomous County, a place with tea civilization.As my parents needed to work at a fixed time and had no time to take care of me, I grew up with my grandparents before I went to school.
My grandparents are farmers with very low educational background, but they still taught me a lot in my ignorant age with practical actions. They taught me to love work,to work diligently, to fear hardship and not to fear fatigue, and to cherish food, whether poor or rich.

Primary School


Start to School

In 2013, when I was five years old, it was time for starting to school. I came to Moshi , 60KM away from where i was born, a small town where my parents worked. This is my second home, the warm harbour when I am tired. I am now studying outside. When I think of it, there will be a warm place in my heart.

My primary school is Liujiayuan Primary School, two kilometers from my home. In the first two years of school, my father used his motorcycle to pick up and deliver school every morning and evening. However, as I grow older, I prefer to walk to school on my own, and I prefer to meet my classmates on the way to school with a sense of laughter, jollification and liveliness. I like to see the quiet mountains in the morning, and I like to run in the evening near the stalks of large rape fields.
